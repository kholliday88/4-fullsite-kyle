Our Story

A man, a tie, a legacy.

In 1971, a man had a dream. That man's name was Roger "Skinny" Sonnheim.

From humble beginnings, Roger started selling his slimmer, more aesthetically pleasing ties out of the trunk of his car. Now, with more than 150 stores in the United States of America, his legacy lives on. Roger believed in two principles in life - hard work and looking good. For all the stylish hard workers out there, make your tie a representation of yourself - make it a Skinny Tie.

As a fast-growing company, Skinny Ties has been listed in the Inc. 500 since 2014. We believe in the power of the American dream. That's why we're proud to annouce that we are fully owned and operated exclusively in America, since 1971.
